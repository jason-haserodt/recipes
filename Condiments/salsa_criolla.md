# Salsa Criolla

_Cuisine:  Peruvian_<br />
_Course:  Condiments_

## Ingredients

- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 medium tomatoes, cored and thinly sliced
- 2 jalapenos, seeded and julienned
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 sprigs cilantro, chopped
- salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Halve the **red onion** and thinly slice.  Place in a medium bowl of ice water and soak for 10 minutes.
1. Core and thinly slice the **tomatoes**.  Seed and thinly slice the **jalapenos**.
1. Wash and chop the **cilantro**.
1. Drain the **red onion slices** and mix in the sliced **tomatoes**, **jalapenos**, **lime juice**, **olive oil**, and **cilantro**.
1. Season with **salt and pepper**.
