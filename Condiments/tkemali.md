# Tkemali

_Cuisine:  Georgian_<br />
_Course:  Condiments_

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs plums
- 1/4 cup water
- 3/4 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp fennel
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 tsp cayanne
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp mint, minced
- 1/3 cup cilantro, minced

## Instructions

1. Cut **plums** in half, remove pits.
1. Place in sauce pan with **water** and bring to a boil.
1. Simmer, covered for 15 minutes.
1. With a morter and pestle, pound **spices** and **garlic** into a paste.
1. When **plums** are soft, puree in a food processor.
1. Return to pan and boil for 3 minutes.
1. Add **spice paste**, cook for 5 minutes.
1. Stir in **herbs**, remove from heat.
1. Pour into a jar, refrigerate.
