# Amarillo Chili Sauce

_Cuisine:  Peruvian_<br />
_Course:  Condiments_

## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp amarillo chili paste
- 3.5 tbsp mayonnaise
- 2 tsp red wine vinegar
- 2 tsp vegetable oil
- 2 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1 pinch dried oregano
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Combine **all ingredients** in a small bowl and mix well.
