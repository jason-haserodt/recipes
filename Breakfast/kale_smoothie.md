# Kale Smoothie

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Breakfast_

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup frozen mango
- 1/2 banana
- 1 knob ginger, roughly chopped
- 1/2 lemon, juiced
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 2 tsp matcha powder
- 4 tbsp yoghurt
- 4 handfulls kale
- 3/4 cup milk

## Instructions

1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
1. Puree until smooth.
