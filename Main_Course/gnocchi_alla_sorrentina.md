# Gnocchi alla Sorrentina

_Cuisine:  Italian_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 1 pack of gnocchi, 1 lbs
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2-3 tbsp butter
- 2 pints cherry tomatoes
- 1 tbsp bamba sauce
- 12-16 oz fresh mozzerella
- chopped basil

## Instructions

1. Mince **garlic** and set aside.  Wash **tomatoes** and set aside.
1. Fry **gnocchi**, covered, in 2 tbsp **olive oil** for 5 minutes.
1. Remove **gnocchi** and add **butter** to brown.
1. Add **minced garlic** and cook for 30 seconds.
1. Add **tomatoes** and cook until they pop, about 8 minutes.
1. Stir in **gnocchi** and top with **mozzerella**.
1. Transfer to the oven and broil until **mozzerella** begins to brown.
1. Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, and top with a garnish of chopped **basil**.
