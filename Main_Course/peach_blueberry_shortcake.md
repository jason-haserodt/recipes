# Peach Blueberry Shortcake

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup butter
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 5 tbsp sugar, divided
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 3/4 cup milk
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 3 cups blueberries, divided
- 1 lbs peaches
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 450 F.
1. Meanwhile, pit and slice the **peaches** and combine with **2 cups blueberries** in a large bowl.
1. Add **3 tbsp sugar**, **balsamic vinegar**, and **1 cup blueberries** to a sauce pan and bring to a boil, about 1 minute.
1. Simmer until blueberries pop and the mixture forms a sauce, 1 or 2 more minutes.
1. Pour the sauce over the fruit and add **black pepper**.
1. Let stand for 30 minutes while the shortcake is being prepared.
1. Mix **flour**, **2 tbsp sugar**, **baking powder**, and **salt** in a large bowl.
1. Cut in **butter** until mixture looks like fine crumbs.
1. Stir in **milk** until just blended.
1. Form dough into a ball and knead 20 to 25 times.
1. Grease an 8" round cake pan and press the dough into the pan.
1. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until golden brown.
