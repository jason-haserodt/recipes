# Green Chile Turkey Soup

_Cuisine:  Tex-Mex_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp oregano
- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 6 tomatillos, chopped
- 2 cans corn
- 1 4oz can green chilis
- 4 cups shredded turkey or chicken
- 6 cups turkey or chicken stock
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- juice from 1 lime
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

## Instructions

1. Chop **onions** and **tomatillos**.  Wash and chop **cilantro**.
1. Measure 4 cups of **cooked turkey** and shred in a bowl with two forks.
1. Saute **onions** and **tomatillos** with **spices** in a large pot for 6 minutes.
1. Add **stock**, **corn**, and **chilis**.
1. Bring to a boil and add **turkey**.
1. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
1. Remove from heat and add **cilantro**, **lime juice**, **salt** and **pepper**.
