# Caramelized Cabbage and Noodles

_Cuisine:  Hungarian_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 1 large head green cabbage
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
- 12 oz wide egg noodles
- salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Core **cabbage**, then halve, and slice very thinly.
1. Place sliced **cabbage** in a colander and sprinkle generously with **salt**.
1. Place a small plate on top of the **cabbage** and top with a weight.
1. Let sit for 1 hour.
1. Rinse **cabbage** and squeeze very dry with your hands.
1. Melt **butter** in a very large skillet.
1. Add **cabbage** and cook over medium-low heat, stirring often, until dark brown and very soft, about 1 hour.
1. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
1. Add **noodles** and cook for 10 minutes, until tender.
1. Drain and shake dry.
1. Add the **noodles** to the **cabbage**, stir and add **salt** and lots of course, freshly ground **black pepper**.
