# Svanetian Meat Pie

_Cuisine:  Georgian_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

**Dough**
- 2.25 tsp yeast
- 1/2 cup warm milk
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 egg, lightly beaten
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 1/4 cup sunflower seed oil
- 4 cups flour

**Filling**
- 1.5 lbs ribeye steak
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp each of coriander, fenugreek, dill, ground caraway seed
- 1/2 tsp black pepper, cayanee
- 1.25 tsp salt
- 4 tbsp butter, cold
- 1 tbsp butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Dissolve **yeast** in **milk** with **sugar**.
1. When foamy, add **water**, **egg**, and **oil**.
1. Place **flour** in a stand mixer bowl and stir in the **liquid mixture**.
1. Knead into a dough and let rise for 1.5-2 hours, until doubled.
1. Dice **ribeye** into 1/4 inch pieces.  Transfer **diced ribeye** to a bowl.
1. Finely chop **onion** and mince **garlic**.  Add to the bowl with the **diced ribeye**.
1. Grind **caraway seeds** and add, along with the other **spices** and **salt** to the bowl.  Stir to combine.
1. Set aside **filling** for 30 minutes.  Preheat oven to 400F.
1. Divide **dough** into four pieces.  Let rest for 10 minutes.
1. Roll out each **dough piece** to an 8 inch round.
1. Using a 3/4 cup measuring cup, place 3/4 cup of **filling** on each round.
1. Add 1 tbsp **butter** bits to each.
1. Pinch edges of **dough** and fold up over the **filling**.  Pinch at the top to seal.
1. Flip **filled dough** over and gently roll to 6 inches diameter.
1. Make a small hole in the center to vent steam while baking.
1. Melt **butter** and bruch each **prepared pie**.
1. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
1. Remove from oven and cover with a kitchen towel and let rest for 10 minutes.
