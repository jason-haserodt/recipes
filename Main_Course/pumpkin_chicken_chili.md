# Pumpkin-Chicken Chili

_Cuisine:  Tex-Mex_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 2 cans pumpkin puree
- 2 cans great northern beans, drained
- 25 oz canned chicken in broth
- 24 oz salsa verde

## Instructions

1. Add **all ingredients** to a slow cooker.
2. Heat on low.
