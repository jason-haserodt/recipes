# Octopus and Huachana Sausage Skewers

_Cuisine:  Peruvian_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 1 large octopus, 2.5-4.5 lbs
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp black peppercorns
- 10.5 oz Peruvian chorizo, cut in 1" slices
- 1 portion amarillo sauce
- 1 portion salsa criolla
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tbsp amarillo chili paste
- 1/4 cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Put **octopus**, **garlic**, **bay leaf**, and **peppercorns** in a deep sauce pan.
1. Cover and cook on low heat for 2 hours.
1. Remove **octopus** and let cool.
1. Cut off head and discard.  Cut **octopus tenticles** into 1.25" chunks.
1. Put marinade ingredients - **2 cloves garlic**, **1 tsp oregano**, **1 tbsp amarillo chili paste**, and **1/4 cup olive oil** - in a bowl and mix well.  Add **cooked octopus chunks** and **sausage chunks** and leave for 10 minutes.
1. Assemble skewers, alternative **octopus** and **sausage**.
1. Cook **prepared skewers** under a broiler for 5 minutes, turning regularly.
1. Serve with **amarillo sauce** and **salsa criolla**.
