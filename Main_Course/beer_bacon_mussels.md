# Beer-Bacon Mussels

_Cuisine:  Belgian_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 4 strips bacon, chopped
- 1 large shallot, minced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 springs thyme
- 3/4 cup belgian ale or wit
- 2 lbs mussels
- 2 tsp mustard
- salt and pepper
- 1/4 cup chopped parsley
- crusty bread

## Instructions

1. Cook **bacon** in large pot.  Remove, let cool, chop.
1. Add **shallots** and **garlic**, cook until soft, 3-5 minutes.
1. Add **thyme** and **beer**.  WHen **beer** bubbles, reduce heat to low.
1. Add **mussels**, cover, and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Remove **cooked mussels**.  Cover again, if necessary, and cook for 5 more minutes.
1. After transfering **cooked mussels** to bowl, whisk in **mustard**, **salt**, and **pepper**.
1. Pour **sauce** over **mussels** and garnish with **bacon** and **parsley**.
1. Serve with **bread**.
