# Grilled Octopus over Grits with Chipotle Aioli

_Cuisine:  Mexican_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 1-2 lbs octopus

**Aioli**
- 3/4 cup mayo
- 2 minced chipotle peppers, plus sauce
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced

**Grits**
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 3 cups whole milk
- 2 tsp salt
- 3/4 tsp seasoning salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 cup stone ground grits
- 2 tbsp butter

**Rub**
- 2 tsp ancho chili powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp oregano
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- sunflower seed oil
- cilantro to garnish

## Instructions

1. Blanch **octopus** in boiling water for 30 seconds.
1. Transfer to an ice bath.
1. Cut individual **tenticles** off of the head and place in a sous vide bag and seal.
1. Sous vide at 175F for 5 hours.
1. Transfer to an ice bath.
1. Refrigerate on a rack, uncovered, for 3 hours.
1. Cover and continue to refrigerate until ready to grill.
1. **Aioli**: Stir together **aioli ingredients**.  Add more **chipotle** to taste.
1. **Grits**: Bring **chicken broth**, **milk**, **salts**, and **sugar** to a boil in a pot over medium heat.
1. Vigerously whisk in **grits** until mixture comes back to a boil.
1. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 45 minutes, stiring frequently.
1. Stir in **butter**, remove from heat, cover, and set aside until **octopus** is ready.
1. **Grill**: Drizzle **oil** over **octopus** and gently toss to coat.
1. Mix the **rub spices** together in a small bowl.
1. Gently coat with the **octopus** with the **rub mix**.
1. Rub **oil** on grill grate.
1. Grill **octopus** for 4 minutes per side.
1. Serve over **grits** with **aioli** and garnish with **cilantro**.
