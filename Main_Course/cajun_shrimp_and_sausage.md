# Cajun Shrimp and Sausage

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 3 teaspoons sweet paprika
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 2 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
- 2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne, adjust to taste for heat or omit
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes, adjust to taste for heat or omit
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 12 oz Kielbasa sausage, sliced into half-moons
- 2-4 teaspoons cooking oil
- 1/2 cup onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 2 cup chicken broth
- 2 cup fresh tomato, diced, from about 1 medium tomato
- 1 red bell pepper, cored and seeded and cut into 1/2-inch chunks
- 1 lb raw or cooked shrimp, thawed
- 1/2 cup half and half cream
- 4 Tablespoons cornstarch
- 4 Tablespoons cold water
- 1/2 cup fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped, plus more for garnish

## Instructions

1. Prepare cajun spice mix by combining all the **spices** in a small bowl. Set aside.
1. Slice the **kielbasa**, dice the **onion**, chop the **garlic**, dice the **tomato**, and chop the **bell pepper**.  Set aside.
1. Add the **kielbasa** to a skillet over medium-high heat. Arrange the pieces so they are all flat in the skillet. Cook until golden underneath then flip them over and brown the other side. 
1. Remove the **sausage** to a bowl and set aside.
1. Heat the **cooking oil** in the same skillet over medium heat. 
1. Add the **onion** and cook, stirring, until softened, about 3 minutes. 
1. Add the **garlic** and cook about 1 minute more. 
1. Add the **Cajun spice mix** and cook with the **onions** briefly. 
1. Add the **tomatoes**, **red bell pepper** and **chicken broth** and stir together.
1. Add the **shrimp** to the skillet. If using raw shrimp, simmer the shrimp in the broth until cooked, about 4 minutes. If using cooked shrimp, cook in the broth just until warmed.
1. Return the **kielbasa** to the skillet and cook briefly to warm. 
1. Add the **cream** and stir in. 
1. Mix together the **cornstarch** and **cold water** in a small bowl. Drizzle into the sauce a bit at a time, stirring constantly, adding as much of the **cornstarch mixture** as needed to thicken the sauce to the desired level.
1. Take a minute to taste the sauce and add additional **salt** and freshly **black pepper** as needed. 
1. Right before serving, stir the **parsley** or **cilantro** into the skillet. 
1. Serve with a wedge of **cornbread**. 
1. Garnish with additional **parsley** or **cilantro**.
