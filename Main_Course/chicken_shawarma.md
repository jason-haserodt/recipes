# Chicken Shawarma

_Cuisine:  Middle Eastern_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp corn starch
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cloves
- 1 tsp cayanne
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp sunflower seed oil
- 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- lavash
- yogurt

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350F.
1. Mix **spices** with **corn starch** in a small bowl.
1. Put **oil** in a large bowl, then add **chicken**, and toss coat.
1. Stir in the **spice mix** and coat the **chicken**.
1. Grease a bread pan and layer and press the **prepared chicken** into it.
1. Bake for 40 minutes.
1. Let **chicken** rest for 10 minutes.
1. Drain any excess liquid, then turn the **chicken** onto a cutting board and slice.
1. Serve with **lavash** and **yogurt**.
