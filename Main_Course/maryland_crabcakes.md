# Maryland Crabcakes

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 16oz lumb crab meat
- 1/4 tsp worchestershire sauce
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp old bay seasoning
- 1/4 tsp ground mustard
- 4 saltine crackers, crumbled
- 4 tbsp mayo
- 1/2 tsp lemon zest
- 2 tbsp butter
- 4 brioche buns
- lettuce
- tartar sauce

## Instructions

1. Mix **all ingredients** except for **butter** and the other remaining ingredients in a large bowl.
1. Form **batter** into four 1" thick patties.
1. Lightly brown for 5 minutes per side on a **buttered** pan or grittle.
1. Serve hot on a **brioche bun** with **lettuce** and **tatar sauce**.
