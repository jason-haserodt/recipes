# Everything Salmon with Creamy Caper Sauce

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 1 small red onion, halved and thinly sliced
- 1 lemon
- salt
- 4 tbsp sunflower seed oil
- 2 medium beefsteak tomatoes, halved and thinly sliced
- 1/3 cup everything but the bagel seasoning
- 4 skin-on salmon fillets (6-8 oz each)
- 4 oz cream cheese
- 2 tbsp capers
- 2 tbsp caper brine

## Instructions

1. Halve and thinly slice the **red onion** and place in a medium bowl with cold water.
1. Swish **onion slices** in water and drain.  Repeat one more time with more water.
1. Halve and thinly slice the **tomatoes**.
1. Zest **lemon**, reserving **zest** for later.
1. Add 1 tbsp **lemon juice** (from half of a lemon), a pinch of **salt**, and 1 tbsp **oil**.
1. Massage **onion mixture** with your hands until soft and pink, then add **tomato slices** and mix to combine.  Add more **salt**, if necessary.
1. Pour the **everything spice mix** into a plate.
1. Cut **salmon** into four pieces.  Season **salmon** with salt on all non-skin sides.
1. Push each non-skin side of each **salmon** into the **everything spice mix** to coat each side.
1. Heat remaining 3 tbsp **oil** in a skillet over medium heat.
1. Add **salmon** skin side down and sear until skin is crispy, 4-5 minutes.
1. Lower heat to medium-low.  Flip **salmon** and sear, seasoning-side down, until crisp, but not burnt, 2-3 minutes.
1. Using tongs, sear **salmon** on the sides briefly too.
1. Transfer **salmon** to a plate and tent with **foil**.
1. Wipe skillet with a papertowl and return to low heat.
1. Break **cream cheese** into chunks and place in the skillet.
1. Add **capers**, **brine**, 1 tbsp **lemon juice** (from the remaining lemon half), 1 tsp **lemon zest**, and 3 tbsp **water**.
1. Using a spatula, vigerously mix until the **cream cheese** has melted and the sauce is creamy, about 3 minutes.
1. Spread one quarter of the **sauce** on each plate and top with **cooked salmon** and **onion-tomato mixture**.

_Cuisine:  Armenian_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1/5" chunks
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 1/2 tsp cayanne
- 1/2 tsp tumeric
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp marigold powder
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- lavash, for serving
- yogurt, for serving
- sumac, to garnish
- parsley, to garnish

## Instructions

1. Combine all the **marinade ingredients**, **onions**, and **chicken** in a ziplock bag, marinate, and refrigerate for 3-12 hours.
1. Pre-heat broiler on low.
1. Thread **chicken** onto skewers and place on a rack on top of a baking sheet.  Reserve marinade and onions.
1. Grill 3-6" from the heat, turning and basting periodically with the reserved marinade until browned all over.
1. Meanwhile, saute **onions** in a skillet.  Set aside.
1. Chop a handful of **parsley** and set aside.
1. Transfer **chicken** from skewers to a serving platter and top with the **sauted onions**.
1. Serve with **chopped parsley**, **yogurt**, **sumac**, and **lavash**.
