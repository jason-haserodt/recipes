# Chicken with Orange and Fennel

_Cuisine:  Mediterranean_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 2 oranges
- pinch of saffron or marigold
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 large fennel bulbs, cut in wedges
- 2.5 lbs bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- black pepper

## Instructions

1. Zest one **orange** and put **orange zest** into a large bowl.
1. Slice half of the **unzested orange** and set aside.
1. Juice the rest of the **oranges** and add **orange juice** to the large bowl.
1. Whisk in **saffron**, 2 tbsp **olive oil**, and 1/2 tsp **salt** in the **orange juice** in the bowl.
1. Pre-heat oven to 425F.
1. Slice **fennel**, toss in **orange juice mixture**, and arrange on a foil lined baking sheet.  Pour the rest of the **orange juice mixture** on top.
1. Place **chicken** on the sheet, brush with **olive oil**, and apply **salt** and **pepper**.
1. Place reserved **orange slices** on top of the **chicken**.
1. Bake for 40 minutes until chicken juices are clear.
1. Remove **orange slices** and broil **chicken** for 3 minutes to crisp the **chicken skin**.
