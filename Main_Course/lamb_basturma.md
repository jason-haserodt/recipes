# Lamb Basturma

_Cuisine:  Georgian_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 2 cups pomegranate juice
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- freshly ground black pepper
- 1 bay leaf, crushed
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 2 lbs lamb, cut into 2" cubes
- 1 lbs eggplant, cubed

## Instructions

1. Place **lamb** in a ziplock bag in a large bowl.
1. Add **pomegranate juice**, **olive oil**, **salt**, **pepper**, **bay leaf**, and **garlic** to the bag.
1. Refrigerate overnight.
1. Cube **eggplant** and parboil in boiling salted water for 4-6 minutes.
1. Cool on cutting board.
1. Place **marinated lamb** and **parboiled eggplant**, alternating, on skewers.
1. Grill over hot coals for 10 minutes.
1. Flip and cook for 10 minutes more.
1. Serve with tkemali plum sauce.
