# Deconstructed Kebab

_Cuisine:  Middle Eastern_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 2 cups greek yogurt
- 2 persian cucumbers
- 3 tbsp mint, finely chopped
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 lbs ground beef or lamb
- 1 small onion
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tsp salt, divided
- freshly ground pepper
- 1/2 cup walnut pieces or pinenuts
- 2 tbsp raisins or dried cherries
- pomegranate molasses
- pitas

## Instructions

1. Course grate **cucumber** and combine with **yogurt**, **chopped mint**, and pressed **garlic** in a bowl.  Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
1. Measure out **dried fruit** and **nuts** and set aside in a small bowl until called for.
1. Chop **onion** and combine with **ground meat**, **turmeric**, 1 tsp **salt**, and a lot of **black pepper** in a large bowl.  Mix well to combine.
1. Heat a large heavy-bottomed pan over medium heat with a little bit of oil.
1. Once the pan is hot, divide the **meat mixture** into large, irregular chunks and drop them into the pan.
1. Cook, undisturbed, until charred on the bottom and browned halfway up the side, about 6-8 minutes.
1. Break up **meat chunks** with a spatula and add the **dried fruit** and **nuts**, stirring frequently until meat is cooked through, about 2-3 minutes.
1. Transfer **meat mixture** to a serving bowl.
1. Remove **cucumber yogurt** from the refrigerator and stir in 1 tsp **salt**.
1. To eat, slather **cucumber yogurt** inside a **pita**, then add **meat mixture** and top with a drizzle of **pomegranate molasses**.
