# Szechuan Eggplan

_Cuisine:  Chinese_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs jaapanese eggplant
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- 2-4 tbsp sunflower seed oil
- 4 garlic cloves, chopped
- 2 tsp ginger, minced
- 5-10 dried red chilis

**sauce**
- 1.5 tsp szechuan peppercorns
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1 tbsp siracha sauce
- 1 tbsp sunflower seed oil
- 1 tbsp Asian cooking wine
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp five spice

## Instructions

1. Cut **eggplant** into 1/2" thick halfmoons or bite-sized pieces.
1. Place **cut eggplant** in bowl of water with **salt**.
1. Cover with a plate and let stand for 20-30 minutes.
1. **Sauce**: Toast **szechuan peppercorns** in a dry skillet for 1-2 minutes.
1. Crush and mix with the **remaining sauce ingredients**.
1. Drain **eggplant** and pat dry.
1. Toss **eggplant** with **cornstarch**.
1. Working in batches, fry **eggplant** in a large flat-bottomed skillet with **oil** until golden brown, about 10 minutes per batch.  Don't rush!  Set aside.
1. Add more **oil** and saute **garlic**, **ginger**, and **chilis** for 2 minutes.
1. Pour in **sauce** and simmer for 20 minutes.
1. Add **eggplant** and gently toss for 1-2 minutes.
1. Serve over **rice** with **peanuts** and **scallions** on top.
