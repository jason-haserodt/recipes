# Cranberry Pork Tenderloin

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Main Course_

## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp butter
- 1.5 cups onion, chopped
- 1 tsp garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp corn starch
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 pork tenderloins (2 lbs)
- 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1/4 tsp cayanne pepper powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp fresh rosemary, minced
- 2 cups fresh cranberries

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 375F.
1. Chop **onion** and mince **garlic**.  Mince **rosemary** and set aside in a small bowl.
1. Measure out **cranberries** and wash.  Set aside.
1. Brown **onions** and **garlic** in a skillet with **2 tbsp butter**.
1. Transfer to a bowl and toss with **cornstarch**.
1. Brown **pork tenderloins** in the skillet with **1 tbsp butter**.
1. Transfer to a 11"x7" glass baking dish.
1. Deglaze skillet with **balsamic vinegar**.
1. Stir in **onion mixture**, **water**, **sugar**, **cayanne**, **salt**, **rosemary**, and **cranberries**.
1. Cook until **sauce** thickens and cranberries pop.
1. Pour **sauce** over **pork** and roast until **pork** is 155F, about 25 minutes.
1. Baste every 10 minutes.
