# Lemon Pie

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Dessert_

## Ingredients

- 1 cups flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/3 cup butter (10 2/3 tbsp)
- 3-5 tbsp cold water
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup flour
- 1 cup hot water
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 pinch salt
- 3 egg whites
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350 F, and prepare the crust, combining **flour** and **salt** in a large bowl.
1. Using a pastry cutter, cut in **butter** until the size of peas.
1. Toss in the **cold water** and, using your hands, form into a dough.
1. Dust a pastry cloth with flour.  Roll out and line a pie pan.
1. Prick the **unbaked pie crust** with a fork and bake for 20 minutes.
1. In a pot, stir together **sugar**, **flour**, and **hot water**.  Cook over medium heat until everything disolves.
1. Beat **egg yolks** with **cold water** and add to the pot.
1. Stir in **lemon juice**, **butter**, and **salt**.  Stir until thick.  Remove from heat.
1. Beat **egg whites**, **sugar**, and **salt** for about 5-10 minutes until peaks form.
1. Pour **lemon custard** into the **baked pie shell** and top with the **maringue**.
1. Bake **prepared pie** for 10 minutes.
1. Cool for 10 minutes.
