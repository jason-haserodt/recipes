# Sauerkraut

_Cuisine:  German_<br />
_Course:  Side Dish_

## Ingredients

- 1 head of green or red cabbage (2.2 kg)
- 225g apples, cored and grated
- 22 coriander seeds, crushed
- 15 juniper seeds, crushed
- 45g salt

## Instructions

1. Core and slice the **cabbage** and place in a large bowl.
1. Crush the **coriander** and **juniper** and add to the **sliced cabbage**.
1. Add **salt**.
1. Grate the **apple** overtop the **sliced cabbage** and mix together.
1. Using the end of a wooden rolling pin, bash the **cabbage mixture** for 10 minutes.
1. Place **cabbage mixture** in a large glass jar and push down to eliminate air bubbles and make liquid rest on top.
1. Add a glass weight to keep the cabbage down and seal the jar with an air lock.
1. Place jar in a warm location, without direct sunlight.
1. Let ferment for 5-10 days.
1. Remove airlock and place in the refrigerator.
1. Eat within two weeks.
