# Broccoli Salad

_Cuisine:  Middle Eastern_<br />
_Course:  Side Dish_

## Ingredients

- 1 large head broccoli
- 300g yogurt
- 4 tbsp sunflower seed oil
- 1 tsp mustard powder
- 1 tbsp mustard seeds
- 1 tsp salt
- fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Submerge **broccoli head** in cool water for 20 minutes to get out any bugs.
1. Drain and cut **florets**.
1. Put **florets** in a large bowl.
1. Put **yogurt** in a medium bowl.
1. Fry **mustard seed powder** and **mustard seeds** in **oil** until seeds start to pop.
1. Pour **mustard oil** into the yogurt, add **salt**, and stir to combine.
1. Pour **yogurt sauce** over the **broccoli**.
1. Chop **parsley** and stir into the **broccoli mixture** to combine.
