# Cauliflower Gratin

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Side Dish_

## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower
- 1 red onion
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp butter
- 2-3 tbsp breadcrumbs
- 4 eggs
- 200g sour cream
- 100g cottage cheese
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of salted water to boil and preheat oven to 392F.
1. Meanwhile, cut **cauliflower** into flourets.
1. Parboil **cauliflower flourets** in boiling water for 5 minutes.
1. Chop **red onion** and fry in **oil**.
1. Grease a souffle dish with **butter** and coat the interior with **bread crumbs**.
1. Crack and separate the four **eggs**, placing the yolks and whites in two medium bowls.
1. Mix **yolks** with **sour cream** and **cottage** cheese.
1. Fold in the **cauliflower** and **onions**.  Season with **salt and papper**.
1. Whisk **egg whites** and fold into the **cauliflower mixture**.
1. Spoon **mixture** into the **prepared souffle dish** and bake for 40 minutes.
1. Serve warm.
