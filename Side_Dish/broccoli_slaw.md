# Broccoli Slaw

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Side Dish_

## Ingredients

- 1 bag broccoli slaw
- 1 bag shredded carrots
- 1/2 walnuts, chopped
- 1/4 cup cranberries or golden raisins
- 1/2 cup italian salad dressing
- 1/4 cup carolina gold bbq sauce

## Instructions

1. Combine **all ingredients** in a large bowl and mix well.
