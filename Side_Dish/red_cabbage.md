# Red Cabbage

_Cuisine:  German_<br />
_Course:  Side Dish_

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- 5 cups (1 head) shredded red cabbage
- 1 cup sliced green apples
- 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
- 3 tbsp water
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 2.25 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp ground cloves

## Instructions

1. Shred **red cabbage** in a food processor.
1. Slice **apples**.
1. Place **butter**, **shredded cabbage**, **sliced apples**, and **sugar** into a large pot.
1. Pour **vinegar** and **water** in and season with **salt**, **pepper**, and **cloves**.
1. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low.
1. Cover and simmer for 2 hours.
