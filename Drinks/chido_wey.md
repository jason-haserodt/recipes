# Chido Wey!

_Cuisine:  Mexican_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 1 oz lemon juice (half lemon)
- 1 tbsp agave
- 2 oz mezcal
- 4 oz tepache
- 3-4 dashes spicy bitters

**Tepache**
- 4.5 cup water
- 1/2 brown sugar
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 4 cloves
- rind and core of 1 pineapple

## Instructions

1. Whisk together **lemon juice**, **agave**, **mezcal**, and **bitters**.
1. Stir in **tepache**.
1. Serve over **ice**.

**Tepache**
1. Dissolve **sugar** in **water**.
1. Add **spices** and **pineapple**.
1. Cover with a towel and ferment for 36 hours.
1. Strain.
