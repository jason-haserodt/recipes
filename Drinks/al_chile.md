# Al Chile!

_Cuisine:  Mexican_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 2 oz mezcal
- 1 oz pinapple juice
- 3/4+ oz ginger syrup
- 1/3 oz lemon juice (half lemon)
- 3-4 dashes spicy bitters
- ice

**Ginger Syrup**
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp minced ginger
- 1 tbsp water

## Instructions

1. Make **ginger syrup** in blender.
1. Combine **all ingredients** in a shaker.
1. Add **ice**, shake.
1. Strain through wire mesh.
