# Hot Toddy

_Cuisine:  British_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 1 oz bourbon or brandy
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tsp lemon juice (from 1 lemon)
- 1/4 cup boiling water

## Instructions

1. Combine **bourbon**, **honey**, and **lemon juice** in a mug.
1. Add **hot water** and stir until **honey** has dissolved.
