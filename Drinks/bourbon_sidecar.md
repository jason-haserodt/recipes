# Bourbon Sidecar

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 2 oz bourbon
- 1 oz triple sec
- 0.5 oz lemon juice (1/4 of a lemon)

## Instructions

1. Peel a strip of **lemon peel** from the **lemon**.  Set aside.
1. Add **bourbon**, **triple sec**, and **lemon juice** to a shaker with **ice**.
1. Shake.
1. Strain.
1. Serve with **lemon peel** garnish.
