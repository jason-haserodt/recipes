# French 75

_Cuisine:  French_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 2 oz gin
- 2 oz triple sec
- 2 oz lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 6 oz prosecco
- ice

## Instructions

1. Mix **gin**, **triple sec**, **lemon juice**, **sugar**, and **ice** in a shaker.
1. Shake vigerously.
1. Strain and pour into glasses.
1. Add **prosecco**.
