# That's My Jam

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- Sprig of mint
- 3/4 oz lemon juice
- 2+ oz bourbon
- 1+ tbsp jam
- 1/2 oz black tea simple syrup
- 2 oz carbonated water

## Instructions

1. Put **lemon juice**, **bourbon**, **mint** in a sharker and muddle.
1. Add **jam** and **black tea simple syrup** with **ice** and shake.
1. Strain and divide between two glasses.
1. Top each glass with 1 oz **carbonated water**.
