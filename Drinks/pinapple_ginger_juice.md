# Pineapple Ginger Juice

_Cuisine:  Jamaican_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 4 cups pineapple chunks from one medium pineapples
- 2 inches ginger root, chopped
- 1 lemon
- 1 cup ice
- 2 cups water
- 2-3 tbsp agave syrup

## Instructions

1. Add **pineapple** and **ginger** to a blender.
1. Squeeze **lemon** over top.
1. Add **ice**, **water**, and **agave syrup**.
1. Blend until smooth.  Add more **agave syrup**, if necessary.
1. Filter through a sieve and serve.
