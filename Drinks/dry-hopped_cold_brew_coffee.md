# Dry-Hopped Cold-Brewed Coffee

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 150g ground coffee
- 1.2 L water
- 0.2 oz hops

## Instructions

1. In the evening, around 6pm, combine **coffee grounds** and **water** in a glass pitcher with a lid.
1. Refrigerate for 16 hours.
1. Strain out the grounds.
1. Add **hops** and refrigerate for 3 days.
1. Strain out **hops**.
