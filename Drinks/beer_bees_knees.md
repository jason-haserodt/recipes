# Beer Bee's Knees

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 1 oz lemon juice
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 oz gin
- 4 oz Belgian triple
- ice

## Instructions

1. Whisk together **lemon juice**, **honey**, and **gin** in a glass measuring cup.
1. Pour over **ice**.
1. Top with **beer**.
