# Black Manhattan

_Cuisine:  American_<br />
_Course:  Drinks_

## Ingredients

- 2 oz bourbon
- 1 oz amaro
- 1 tsp marccino cherry syrup
- 2 dash orange bitters
- 2 dash black walnut bitters
- 1 marccino cherry

## Instructions

1. Add **ice** to a glass measuring cup.
1. Add **all of the ingredients**.
1. Stir until chilled, about 20 seconds.
1. Strain into a glass with a **large ice cube**.
1. Add a **marccino cherry** and serve.
